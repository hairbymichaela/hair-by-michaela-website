About Me

Hi, I'm Michaela.

I want every appointment to feel calm, friendly and completely tailored to you.

After working in the same salon from the age of 17, I took the leap and started Hair by Michaela—creating a business that reflects the experience I want every client to have.

Whether you're ready for a full colour change or simply want your hair to feel like you again, you will always get honest advice, careful attention and no pressure.
